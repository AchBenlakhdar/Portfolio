About me

Hello and welcome to my Portfolio where you will be provided with some information about me.

I'm Achraf Benlakhdar, 20 years old Computer Science student at the Internation Academy of Civil Aviation (AIAC). I'm also an IEEE AIAC Student Branch active member. Since middle school I started to get interested in Science, especially Mathematics and Physics. Fascinated by the Polymath mindset, I started learning about many other fields like psychology, philosophy,biology, etc. But the domain that fascinated me the most was Computer Science. Practicing it made me learn how to be a hardworking person and how to truly manage my time. Thus, gaining experience and knowledge in that field was and still is my goal since back then.

But one piece was still missing. Beyond academical studies there is a whole undescovered world of professional work. Transforming the World by solving big problems, Making a change and helping others , Working on big projects with interesting people and all the other exciting goals that I set for my professional career make me insanly excited to discover this new world. This is why I'm genuinely so happy to work with any reputable organization that will help me enhance my knowledge in the IT sector as well as learn from industry-leading professionals.
